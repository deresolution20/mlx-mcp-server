2 The 3 Universal Pain Points
Every successful AI monetization channel solves at least one of these — Hermes solves all three
Pain Point #1
No Soul, No Return Visitors
95% of AI content is a pipeline with no identity. Generic voices, generic images, nothing to come back for. Audiences follow characters, not feeds. Without a persistent persona, every post starts from zero.
⚡ Average retention on soulless AI channels: <30 seconds. Channels with a defined persona: 4–6 minutes.
Pain Point #2
Platform Dependency & Policy Risk
YouTube demonetizes. Spotify rejects. Adobe Stock flags AI. Etsy suspends accounts. Every platform is tightening AI content policies. Creators who built entirely on one platform lost everything overnight in 2024.
⚡ 3 major stock platforms began AI rejections in Q1 2025. YouTube's AI disclosure policy affects 40%+ of faceless channels.
Pain Point #3
Saturation Without Differentiation
Every niche (Reddit stories, motivational quotes, lo-fi music) now has thousands of AI channels doing the exact same thing with the same tools. CPMs drop, algorithms stop recommending. The window for "just start one" has mostly closed.
⚡ "AI art account" searches on Instagram: up 800% since 2023. Engagement rate on generic AI art posts: down 65% in same period.
